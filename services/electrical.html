Common Electrical Problems We Fix

⚠️ Circuit Breakers Tripping

Frequent breaker trips indicate overloaded circuits, short circuits, or failing breakers. We diagnose and fix the root cause.

⚠️ Flickering Lights

Flickering can indicate loose connections, overloaded circuits, or voltage issues. We identify and resolve the problem.

⚠️ Outlets Not Working

Dead outlets may have tripped GFCI, loose wiring, or need replacement. We troubleshoot and repair.

⚠️ Aluminum Wiring

Homes with aluminum wiring need special attention. We provide COPALUM connections and copper pigtailing.

⚠️ Outdated Panels

Federal Pacific, Zinsco, and other obsolete panels are safety hazards. We replace with modern, safe panels.

⚠️ Electrical Shocks

Getting shocked by outlets or switches indicates dangerous wiring issues. We diagnose and repair immediately.
