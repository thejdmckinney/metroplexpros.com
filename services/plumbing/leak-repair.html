Professional Leak Detection & Repair Services

Not all water leaks are obvious. Hidden leaks behind walls, under slabs, or underground can waste water, increase bills, and cause serious structural damage. MetroPlex Pros uses advanced leak detection technology to locate leaks quickly without unnecessary demolition, then repairs them right the first time.

Signs You May Have a Water Leak

💰

Unexplained water bill increase

🔊

Sound of running water

💧

Wet spots on walls or ceiling

🦠

Musty odors or mold growth

⏱️

Water meter running when no water is on

🏚️

Foundation cracks or settlement

🔥

Warm spots on floor (hot water leak)

📉

Low water pressure

Our Advanced Leak Detection Methods

🎧

Electronic Leak Detection

State-of-the-art electronic listening devices amplify the sound of water escaping from pipes, pinpointing leak locations with precision.

🌡️

Thermal Imaging

Infrared cameras detect temperature differences caused by water leaks behind walls, under floors, or in ceilings without any invasive work.

📹

Video Pipe Inspection

Waterproof cameras inspect the inside of pipes to locate cracks, blockages, and leak sources in drain and sewer lines.

💪

Pressure Testing

We pressurize your water system to identify leaks and determine the severity of the problem quickly and accurately.

🔬

Tracer Gas Detection

Safe, non-toxic gas is introduced into pipes and detected with specialized equipment to find even the smallest leaks.
